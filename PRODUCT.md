# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Professionals and learners using the SkillFrameX platform. They need a clear, functional interface to track progress and access courses.

## Product Purpose
A professional, product-focused skill learning and tracking platform.

## Brand Personality
Bold, immersive, tech-forward, and engaging.

## Anti-references
Boring, corporate SaaS look, flat design, minimal "safe" light themes.

## Design Principles
- Striking visuals and deep aesthetics
- Engaging user experience with micro-interactions
- High tech feel with neon accents
- High usability and accessibility

## Accessibility & Inclusion
High contrast for readability, accessible to screen readers, responsive on all devices.
